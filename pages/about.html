About Us

El-sayed Farrag Foundation is a prominent organization dedicated to social development and community enrichment. With over two decades of committed service, our foundation has become a cornerstone in the field of philanthropy and social welfare within the Arab Republic of Egypt.

El-sayed Farrag Foundation has been in the foundry business since 1986, specializing in the production of high quality ductile and grey cast iron products. Initially focusing on the production of pipes, fittings, penstocks, general engineering castings, covering the infra structure requirements. The company later diversified its production in order to meet the demands of the construction, water and the wastewater sector. During this period, El-sayed Farrag has demonstrated its ability to continually improve the quality of its products, thus earning itself a prestigious place in the foundry and construction business and becoming a trademark of high quality cast products. Our foundation operates with a deep commitment to quality and adherence to international standards . El-sayed Farrag’s lines of manhole covers, trench drains and gratings have earned its reputation of competing in quality and price with the best internationally acknowledged brands.... Aiming to diversify its production, El-sayed Farrag has been developing new lines of products to cover a wide range of requirements for residential and commercial communities with products such as tree grates, fire hydrants , lamp posts, bollards, street signs and table bases. This expanding range of products does not come to an end, where we continue to encourage the production of custom made castings to fulfill specific market requirements, which ensures that we meet market demands and customer satisfaction, meanwhile enabling us to continue expanding our market base.Over the years, El-sayed Farrag Foundation has made significant strides in addressing key social issues, implementing projects that cater to the diverse needs of our society. From educational scholarships to health awareness campaigns, our efforts are rooted in our core belief that every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive , Our team consists of dedicated professionals and volunteers who work tirelessly to ensure that our mission is realized. With a robust framework and a strategic approach, we continue to drive positive change and foster an environment of growth and prosperity.
